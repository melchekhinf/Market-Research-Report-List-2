<p><p>The Map Positioning Unit market can be categorized into two types based on accuracy: the Centimeter-level Positioning Unit and the Meter-level Positioning Unit markets. The Centimeter-level Positioning Unit provides highly accurate positional information with precision down to the centimeter level. It is widely used in industries such as surveying, agriculture, and autonomous vehicles. On the other hand, the Meter-level Positioning Unit provides less precise positional data with accuracy up to the meter level. It is commonly utilized in applications like navigation systems and asset tracking where lower accuracy is acceptable.</p></p>
