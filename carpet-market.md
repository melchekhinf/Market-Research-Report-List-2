<p><p>The carpet market is diverse and consists of various types. Tufted carpet is produced by tufting yarn into a backing material. Wilton woven carpet is created on specialized looms that interlace the pile and backing yarns together. Woven Axminster carpets are made using a weaving technique that combines various colors and designs. Hand weaving involves creating carpets by hand-knotting yarns onto a warp and weft foundation. Lastly, there are other market types, including machine-made carpets and printed carpets, which offer different styles and production methods.</p></p>
