Various types are available in the market, including Small Form-Factor Pluggable (SFP), Enhanced Small Form-Factor Pluggable (SFP+), Quad Small Form-Factor Pluggable (QSFP/QSFP+), 10 Gigabit Small Form-Factor Pluggable (XFP), and C Form-Factor Pluggable (CXP). SFP transceivers are compact and versatile, supporting various data rates. SFP+ transceivers offer higher bandwidth and faster data rates. QSFP/QSFP+ transmitters enable high-speed data transmission and are used in network switches. XFP transceivers support 10 Gbps data rates and are widely used in telecommunication networks. CXP modules enable high-speed data transfer in data centers and supercomputers.</p></p>
