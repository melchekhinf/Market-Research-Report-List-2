<p><p>The emergency ceiling light market comprises three main types of systems: self-contained power systems, central power systems, and hybrid power systems. Self-contained power systems are standalone units equipped with their own power supply, typically batteries, and are used in small buildings or areas. Central power systems, on the other hand, are connected to a central power source and distribute emergency lighting in larger buildings. Lastly, hybrid power systems combine both self-contained and central power systems to provide a versatile and reliable emergency lighting solution for various applications.</p></p>
