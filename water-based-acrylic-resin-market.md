<p><p>The water-based acrylic resin market can be categorized into three types: acrylic resin emulsion, acrylic resin suspension, and others. Acrylic resin emulsion refers to water-based acrylic resins that are prepared by emulsifying them with water, resulting in a milky white liquid. Acrylic resin suspension, on the other hand, involves suspending the acrylic resin particles in water, creating a translucent liquid. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses alternative methods of water-based acrylic resin production that are not classified as emulsions or suspensions.</p></p>
