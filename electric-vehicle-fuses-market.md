<p><p>The electric vehicle fuses market can be categorized into three types: high voltage fuses, cartridge fuses, and high current fuses. High voltage fuses are designed to protect the vehicle's electrical system from voltage spikes and surges. Cartridge fuses are small, cylindrical fuses that provide protection against overcurrent. They are commonly used in electric vehicles to safeguard various components such as batteries and motors. High current fuses are specifically designed to handle large amounts of current and are essential for protecting high-power electric vehicle systems from overloading or short circuits.</p></p>
