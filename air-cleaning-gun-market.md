<p><p>The Air Cleaning Gun market consists of various types: Concentrated Jet Stream, Medium Flow, Open Flow, and Fan Spray. Concentrated Jet Stream guns produce a focused and powerful stream of air, making them suitable for removing small particles or debris in hard-to-reach areas. Medium Flow guns provide a balanced combination of pressure and airflow, making them versatile for general cleaning purposes. Open Flow guns emit a wide and gentle airflow, ideal for removing dust or drying objects. Fan Spray guns disperse a wider airflow, making them effective for larger surface cleaning or cooling applications.</p></p>
