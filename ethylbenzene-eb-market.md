There are three main market types for EB production: AlCl3 Liquid Hydrocarbon Method, Zeolite Gas Catalystic Method, and Zeolite Liquid Catalystic Method. In the AlCl3 Liquid Hydrocarbon Method, aluminum chloride is used to catalyze the reaction between benzene and ethylene. The Zeolite Gas Catalystic Method employs a zeolite catalyst in a gas-phase reaction, while the Zeolite Liquid Catalystic Method involves a zeolite catalyst in a liquid-phase reaction. These different methods provide alternative approaches to producing EB.</p></p>
