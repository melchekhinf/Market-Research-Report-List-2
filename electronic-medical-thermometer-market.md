<p><p>The electronic medical thermometer market consists of various types, including anal, ear, forehead, oral, and axillary thermometers. Anal thermometers are designed to measure body temperature rectally, while ear thermometers use infrared technology to measure temperature within the ear canal. Forehead thermometers measure temperature by scanning the forehead, while oral thermometers are used by placing the thermometer under the tongue. Axillary thermometers measure temperature by placing the thermometer in the armpit. Each type of thermometer serves a specific purpose in monitoring body temperature accurately and conveniently.</p></p>
