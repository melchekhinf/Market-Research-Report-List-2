<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluid sensors are used to measure and monitor various fluid properties such as level, pressure, and flow rate. They can be classified into two categories: contact and non-contact sensors. Contact sensors come into direct physical contact with the fluid and use mechanical or electrical components to measure the properties. On the other hand, non-contact sensors utilize technologies like ultrasonic waves or optical methods to measure the fluid properties without any physical contact. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and healthcare for accurate fluid monitoring and control.</p></p>
