<p><p>Head-Up Display (HUD) is a technology that presents information to the user without them needing to look away from their usual line of sight. Conventional HUDs are typically used in vehicles and aircraft, providing essential information like vehicle speed and navigation directions. They project this data onto a transparent display within the driver or pilot's field of view. On the other hand, Augmented Reality (AR)-based HUDs integrate digital graphics with the real-world environment, overlaying contextual information such as road signs or directions onto the windshield or visor. This technology enhances situational awareness and safety for the user.</p></p>
