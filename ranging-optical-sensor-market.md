<p><p>Ranging optical sensors are devices used to measure distance or detect objects using light technology. This market consists of two main types: Laser and LED sensors. Laser sensors utilize laser beams to accurately measure distance and detect objects, offering high precision and long-range capabilities. LED sensors, on the other hand, use light emitting diodes to perform similar tasks but with lower precision and shorter range compared to laser sensors. Both sensor types serve diverse applications including robotics, automation, industrial processes, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
