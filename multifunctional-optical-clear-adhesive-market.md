<p><p>The Multifunctional Optical Clear Adhesive (OCA) market comprises two types: Resistive Optical Clear Adhesive and Capacitive Optical Clear Adhesive. Resistive OCA is used in touch screens that are pressure-sensitive, allowing users to interact by applying pressure with their finger or a stylus. Conversely, Capacitive OCA enables touch screens that respond to the electric charge of the user's body, providing a more intuitive and responsive touch experience. Both types of OCA play crucial roles in the rapidly growing market for advanced touch screen devices.</p></p>
