<p><p>Temperature Monitoring Market Types are divided into two categories, namely Non-Contact Temperature Sensors and Contact Temperature Sensors. </p><p>Non-contact temperature sensors use infrared technology to measure temperature from a distance without physically touching the object or person being measured. These sensors are commonly used in industrial settings, medical applications, and for monitoring food safety.</p><p>On the other hand, contact temperature sensors require direct physical contact with the object or person being measured. These sensors typically use thermocouples or resistance temperature detectors (RTDs) to measure temperature accurately. They are commonly used in HVAC systems, automotive industries, and household appliances.</p></p>
