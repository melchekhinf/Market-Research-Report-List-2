<p><p>The cheese market can be classified into various types based on their textures. Soft cheese, like Brie or Camembert, has a creamy texture and is often spreadable. Semi-soft cheese, such as Havarti or Mozzarella, has a slightly firmer texture but is still pliable. Medium-hard cheese, like Cheddar or Gouda, has a firm yet smooth texture. Hard cheese, such as Parmesan or Romano, has a dense and crumbly texture. These different types cater to varying taste preferences and are used in a wide range of culinary applications.</p></p>
