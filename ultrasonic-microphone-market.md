<p><p>The Ultrasonic Microphone market comprises two key types: Touchless Control and Touch Control. Touchless Control refers to microphones that utilize ultrasonic waves to detect and interpret gestures made by individuals without physical touch, enabling hands-free control of devices. This technology finds applications in automotive, consumer electronics, and healthcare sectors, among others. On the other hand, Touch Control involves using ultrasonic microphones to detect and interpret touch gestures, offering an interactive and intuitive user experience. Touch Control is commonly used in applications such as smartphones, tablets, and wearable devices.</p></p>
