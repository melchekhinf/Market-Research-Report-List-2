<p><p>The Low Voltage MOSFET market consists of two major types: N-Channel and P-Channel. In the N-Channel market, the MOSFETs are based on N-type semiconductor material, allowing current to flow from source to drain when a positive voltage is applied. On the other hand, in the P-Channel market, the MOSFETs are based on P-type semiconductor material, enabling current flow from drain to source with a negative voltage applied. Both types cater to various applications, such as electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors, where efficient power management and switching capabilities are crucial.</p></p>
