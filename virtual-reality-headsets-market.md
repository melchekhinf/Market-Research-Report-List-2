<p><p>In the virtual reality headsets market, there are two main types: circumscribed type and integrated type. Circumscribed type headsets require a separate device, such as a smartphone or a computer, to power and run the virtual reality experience. These headsets are often more affordable and provide flexibility in terms of device compatibility. On the other hand, integrated type headsets have built-in processors and displays, offering a self-contained virtual reality experience without the need for additional devices. These headsets usually provide higher quality visuals and a more seamless user experience but tend to be more expensive.</p></p>
