<p><p>The fiber optic displacement sensors market includes different types of sensors such as intensity modulated fiber optic sensors, phase modulated fiber optic sensors, wavelength modulated fiber optic sensors, and polarization modulated fiber optic sensors. Intensity modulated sensors measure displacement by varying the intensity of transmitted light. Phase modulated sensors change the phase of light to measure displacement. Wavelength modulated sensors use changes in wavelength to detect displacement. Polarization modulated sensors rely on the polarization state of light to measure displacement. These sensors cater to different applications and industries, providing accurate and reliable displacement measurements.</p></p>
