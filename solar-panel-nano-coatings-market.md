<p><p>The solar panel nano coatings market includes two types of coatings: hydrophilic and hydrophobic. Hydrophilic coatings have a high affinity for water, allowing it to spread evenly across the surface of the solar panel. This helps to keep the panel clean by minimizing dust and dirt buildup. On the other hand, hydrophobic coatings repel water, creating a self-cleaning effect as any water that comes into contact with the panel simply rolls off, taking away any dust or dirt with it. Both types of coatings contribute to the maintenance and overall efficiency of solar panels.</p></p>
