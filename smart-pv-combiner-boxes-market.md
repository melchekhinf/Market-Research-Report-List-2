<p><p>Smart PV combiner boxes are an essential component in solar power systems that combine multiple photovoltaic (PV) strings and feed them into a single inverter. The market for these boxes is categorized into three types: 4-channel, 8-channel, and 16-channel. The number of channels refers to the number of PV strings that can be connected to the combiner box. A 4-channel combiner box can accommodate four PV strings, an 8-channel box can accommodate eight strings, and a 16-channel box can accommodate sixteen strings. This categorization allows for flexibility and scalability in designing solar power systems according to the required number of PV strings.</p></p>
