<p><p>Cotton fiber filter paper is available in two main types: qualitative and quantitative filter paper. Qualitative filter paper is designed to separate solid particles from liquids based on their size, without precise measurement. It is commonly used in applications where general filtration is required, such as removing impurities from water. On the other hand, quantitative filter paper provides precise measurement by trapping and retaining particles of a specific size, often used for accurate analysis and determining the concentration of substances in a solution.</p></p>
