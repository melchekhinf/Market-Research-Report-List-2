<p><p>Hot-pressed magnets are used in various industries and can be classified into two types: injection magnets and pressed magnets. Injection magnets are produced by injecting molten magnetic material into a mold, which allows for complex shapes and designs. These magnets are widely used in industries such as automotive, electronics, and medical devices. Pressed magnets, on the other hand, are made by pressing powdered magnetic material into a mold under heat and pressure. They are often used in applications that require high magnetic performance, such as motors, generators, and magnetic sensors.</p></p>
