<p><p>The Cotton Shirt Fabrics market consists of two main types: Purified Cotton and Cotton Mixing. Purified Cotton refers to fabrics made entirely from cotton fibers that have been processed and treated to remove impurities. This type offers a high level of comfort and breathability. On the other hand, Cotton Mixing involves blending cotton fibers with other materials like polyester or spandex. This blend enhances the fabric's durability, stretchability, and wrinkle-resistance while still retaining the comfort and softness of cotton. These market types cater to different consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
