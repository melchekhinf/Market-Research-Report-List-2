<p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Devices are used in various applications and can be classified into different market types. Filters are SAW devices that are used to eliminate unwanted frequencies in electronic circuits. Oscillators generate periodic signals of specific frequencies. Resonators are SAW devices that resonate at specific frequencies, used for frequency stabilization. Transducers convert energy from one form to another, such as electrical to mechanical. Other SAW devices include delay lines, frequency multipliers, and sensors. Overall, SAW devices offer efficient and reliable solutions for signal processing and sensing in various industries.</p></p>
