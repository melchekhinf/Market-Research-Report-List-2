<p><p>Absolute magnetic encoders are used to measure and track the position of an object with high accuracy. There are two main types: linear magnetic encoders and rotary magnetic encoders. Linear magnetic encoders are used to detect linear motion, such as in manufacturing and robotics, while rotary magnetic encoders measure rotational motion, commonly found in motor control and navigation systems. Both types utilize magnets and magnetic sensors to provide absolute position information, ensuring precise measurements without the need for referencing or homing.</p></p>
