<p><p>The wearable computing glasses market consists of two main types: built-in sensor and external sensor. Built-in sensor glasses are equipped with sensors integrated directly into the glasses frame. These sensors measure various parameters such as heart rate, body temperature, and motion detection. On the other hand, external sensor glasses rely on sensors that are placed outside the glasses frame, usually on the user's body. These sensors collect data and transmit it to the wearable glasses, enabling functions such as augmented reality and activity tracking. Both types offer different benefits and use cases for users.</p></p>
