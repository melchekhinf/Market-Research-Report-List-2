<p><p>UV-reduced adhesive tapes are classified into two types: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided adhesive tapes have adhesive on one side, allowing them to bond to a single surface or object. They are commonly used for sealing, joining, and packaging applications. Double-sided adhesive tapes have adhesive on both sides, enabling them to bond two surfaces or objects together. These tapes are often used for mounting, bonding, and laminating purposes. Both types of UV-reduced adhesive tapes offer reduced exposure to ultraviolet radiation, making them suitable for applications where UV resistance is required.</p></p>
