<p><p>Crane cables are classified into different types based on their composition and properties. PUR (Polyurethane) cables are known for their high flexibility, resistance to oil and chemicals, and durability in harsh environments. They are commonly used in heavy-duty applications where flexibility and reliability are crucial. PVC (Polyvinyl chloride) cables are cost-effective and have good mechanical and electrical properties. They are suitable for general-purpose applications where moderate flexibility and resistance to abrasion and weather conditions are required. Both PUR and PVC cables play a significant role in the crane cables market, catering to different requirements and budgets.</p></p>
