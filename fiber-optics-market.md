<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Fiber optics market can be classified into two types: multi-mode fiber optics and single-mode fiber optics. Multi-mode fiber optic cables are designed to transmit multiple light signals simultaneously through a larger core, suitable for short-distance transmissions. On the other hand, single-mode fiber optic cables are designed to transmit a single light signal through a smaller core, enabling longer-distance transmissions with higher bandwidth and less signal loss. These two types cater to different communication needs and are widely used in various industries like telecommunications, data centers, and healthcare.</p></p>
