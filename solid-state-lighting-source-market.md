<p><p>The solid-state lighting source market comprises three main types: Light Emitting Diodes (LEDs), Organic Light Emitting Diodes (OLEDs), and Polymer Light Emitting Diodes (PLEDs). LEDs are semiconductor devices that emit light when current passes through them. OLEDs use thin organic layers to emit light and are flexible and lightweight. PLEDs also use organic materials but in the form of polymers, offering low-cost production and improved flexibility. These solid-state lighting sources provide energy-efficient lighting solutions for various applications including residential, commercial, and automotive lighting.</p></p>
