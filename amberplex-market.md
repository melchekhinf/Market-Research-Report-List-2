<p><p>Amberplex is a leading player in the ion exchange membrane market, providing a variety of market types. The cation exchange membrane functions by selectively allowing cations to pass, while blocking anions. Anion exchange membranes work in the opposite manner, allowing anions while blocking cations. Amphoteric ion exchange membranes exhibit both cation and anion exchange properties. Bipolar ion exchange membranes possess both cation and anion exchange groups, providing the ability to selectively transport both types of ions. Mosaic ion exchange membranes are a combination of multiple ion exchange materials, offering enhanced performance in various applications.</p></p>
