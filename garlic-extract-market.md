<p><p>Garlic extract is available in various forms in the market. Garlic powder is dehydrated garlic that has been finely ground. It is commonly used as a seasoning or ingredient in food preparations. Garlic oil is extracted from garlic cloves and is highly concentrated. It is used mainly for medicinal purposes or as a flavoring agent. Garlic paste is made by crushing garlic cloves and mixing them with oil or water. It is convenient to use in recipes. Granulated garlic is made from dried garlic cloves and has a coarse texture. It is widely used as a seasoning for dishes.</p></p>
