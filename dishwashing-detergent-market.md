<p><strong>The Dishwashing Detergent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Saponification</li><li>Non-saponification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dishwashing detergent market can be divided into two types: saponification and non-saponification. Saponification detergents are made through a process called saponification, where natural fats or oils are combined with an alkali to create soap. These detergents are environmentally friendly and biodegradable. Non-saponification detergents, on the other hand, are made from synthetic chemicals and do not undergo the saponification process. They are often more effective in removing tough stains but are not as eco-friendly.</p></p>
