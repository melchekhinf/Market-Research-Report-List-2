<p><p>The SoC Tester Market includes various types of systems: the 6-slot system, 12-slot system, 24-slot system, 36-slot system, and others. The 6-slot system refers to a testing setup that can accommodate up to six System-on-Chip (SoC) devices simultaneously. Similarly, the 12-slot, 24-slot, and 36-slot systems can test twelve, twenty-four, and thirty-six SoCs simultaneously, respectively. Lastly, the "Others" market includes any additional variants that may have different slot numbers. These systems are designed to facilitate efficient testing of SoCs, allowing multiple devices to be tested simultaneously, saving time and enhancing productivity.</p></p>
