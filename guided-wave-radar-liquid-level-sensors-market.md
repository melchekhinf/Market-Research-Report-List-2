<p><p>The Guided Wave Radar Liquid Level Sensors market consists of two main types: Probe Type and Cable Type. The Probe Type sensors utilize a probe that is directly immersed in the liquid, allowing for accurate level measurement. These sensors are suitable for both solid and liquid materials and are widely used in various industries. On the other hand, Cable Type sensors use a cable that is immersed in the liquid, providing continuous measurement along its length. These sensors are commonly used in applications where the liquid level needs to be monitored over a large distance.</p></p>
