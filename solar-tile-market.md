<p><p>The solar tile market can be categorized into two types based on the material used in their construction: Monocrystalline and Polycrystalline. Monocrystalline solar tiles are made from a single crystal structure and are known for their higher efficiency and sleek appearance. On the other hand, Polycrystalline solar tiles are made from multiple crystal structures and are generally less expensive but have a slightly lower efficiency. Both types of solar tiles are designed to harness solar energy and provide an eco-friendly alternative to traditional roofing materials.</p></p>
