<p><p>The Push-Pull fingerprint lock market offers two types: Semi-Automatic and Fully Automatic locks. Semi-Automatic locks require a manual action, such as pushing or pulling the door handle, along with using a fingerprint to unlock the door. These locks are more affordable and suitable for residential use. Fully Automatic locks, on the other hand, automatically open and close the door once the fingerprint is recognized, eliminating the need for manual action. These locks are ideal for commercial or high-traffic areas where convenience and speed are paramount.</p></p>
