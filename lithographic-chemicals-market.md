<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Lithographic Chemicals Market is divided into several types. Removers are chemicals used to eliminate unwanted materials from surfaces during lithographic processes. Deposition precursors are substances that facilitate the deposition of film or layers onto substrates. Etchants are chemicals used to remove certain areas of a substrate, enabling selective etching. Dopants are elements or compounds added to alter the electrical or optical properties of materials. Esists are chemicals that enhance the adherence of materials on substrates. Lastly, Others category encompasses additional chemicals used in lithography that do not fall into the aforementioned types.</p></p>
