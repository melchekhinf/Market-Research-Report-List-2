<p><p>The silk quilt market is divided into two main types: Mulberry Silk and Tussah Silk. Mulberry Silk is considered to be of higher quality and is derived from silkworms fed on a diet of mulberry leaves. It is known for its softness, durability, and luxurious feel, making it highly sought after in the market. On the other hand, Tussah Silk is produced by silkworms that feed on oak leaves and has a slightly coarser texture. It is often preferred for its natural, rustic appeal and is more affordable compared to Mulberry Silk.</p></p>
