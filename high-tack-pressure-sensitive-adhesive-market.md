<p><p>The High Tack Pressure Sensitive Adhesive (PSA) Market has three major types: Solvent-Based, Water-Based, and Hot-Melts. Solvent-Based PSAs are adhesives that use solvents to make them sticky. Water-Based PSAs, on the other hand, rely on water as a carrier to make them tacky. Hot-Melt PSAs are adhesives that solidify upon cooling after application and do not require any solvents or water. These different types of PSAs cater to various application needs, offering a range of adhesive options with varying drying times, environmental impact, and bonding strength.</p></p>
