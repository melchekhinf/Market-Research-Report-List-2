<p><p>Fresh yeast is a type of yeast that is often used in baking and fermentation processes. The market for fresh yeast can be divided into two main types: high sugar type and low sugar type. </p><p>High sugar type yeast is specifically formulated for use in recipes that contain high levels of sugar, such as sweet breads and pastries. This type of yeast is known for its ability to tolerate high sugar concentrations, resulting in better fermentation and rising of the dough.</p><p>On the other hand, low sugar type yeast is more suitable for recipes with low sugar content or those that require longer fermentation times. This type of yeast is designed to provide a slower fermentation process, leading to better flavor development and texture in the final product.</p><p>Both the high sugar type and low sugar type yeasts cater to different baking and fermentation needs, ensuring that bakers and food manufacturers have suitable options depending on their specific requirements.</p></p>
