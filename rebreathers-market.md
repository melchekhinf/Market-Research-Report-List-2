<p><p>Rebreathers are advanced diving equipment that recycle exhaled breath by removing carbon dioxide and replenishing it with oxygen, allowing for extended dive times. The rebreathers market is comprised of two main types: closed circuit rebreathers (CCR) and semi-closed rebreathers (SCR). CCR rebreathers maintain a closed loop system, constantly monitoring the gas mixture and adding oxygen as needed. SCR rebreathers, on the other hand, mix fresh air with exhaled breath before discharging it, resulting in less efficient gas consumption compared to CCR rebreathers. Both types cater to diverse diving needs and offer unique advantages and limitations.</p></p>
