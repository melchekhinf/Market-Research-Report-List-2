<p><p>The InGaAs detector market comprises two main types: Multi InGaAs Photodiodes Arrays and Single InGaAs Photodiodes. Multi InGaAs Photodiodes Arrays consist of multiple individual InGaAs photodiodes integrated on a single chip, allowing for simultaneous detection of multiple wavelengths or positions. They find application in optical communication, spectroscopy, and imaging. On the other hand, Single InGaAs Photodiodes are individual detectors that excel in high-speed performance and offer high responsivity to near-infrared light. They are commonly used in fiber-optic communication systems, optical sensors, and scientific research.</p></p>
